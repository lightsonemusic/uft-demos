About KHAEOS-Russell Module
KHAEOS-Russell integrates pneumatic frequency synthesis with Russell's harmonic periodic table. The system maps acoustic frequencies (Russell octaves 1-8) to pneumatic slot gaps, jet velocities, and power requirements. Each octave creates a unique frequency resonance point optimized for KHAEOS hardware constraints. All values derived from UFT REFERENCE.md — this is not theoretical, it's precise engineering data.
